Destination Highlights

Karachi

Explore the bustling city of Karachi, known for its vibrant markets, beaches, and historical sites like the Quaid-e-Azam Mausoleum.

K2 (Mount Godwin-Austen)

The second-highest mountain in the world, located in the Karakoram range. Perfect for adventure seekers and trekkers.

Travel Tips

  • Transportation: Use trains or buses for domestic travel. International flights arrive in major cities like Lahore and Islamabad.
  • Accommodations: Book hotels or guesthouses in advance. Budget options are available in tourist areas.
  • Safety: Stick to well-lit areas at night. Respect local customs and carry essentials like water and sunscreen.
